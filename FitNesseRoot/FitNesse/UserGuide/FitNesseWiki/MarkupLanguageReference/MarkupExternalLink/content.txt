A link to an external web page is created by typing the URL.

|!c '''Markup Text'''|!c '''Displayed as:'''|
|!-http://www.objectmentor.com-!|http://www.objectmentor.com|

If you would like to create a link to the '''files''' section of the local FitNesse installation, you may omit the domain name.

|!c '''Markup Text'''|!c '''Displayed as:'''|
|!-http://files/images/-!|http://files/images/|
